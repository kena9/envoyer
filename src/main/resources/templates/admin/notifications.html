How it works

Email — sent asynchronously to all active newsletter subscribers. Each email includes a one-click unsubscribe link.

Push — sent to all browsers where users have allowed notifications. Appears even when the tab is closed.

Users subscribe to push via the bell icon shown on the site (guests + logged-in users).
